Uranus

FACT

>>Name: Uranus
>>Distance from sun: 2.9 billion km [1.8 billion miles]
>>Number of known moons: 27
>>Average temperature: -212*C [-350*F]
>>Length of year: 84 earth years

DETAIL

Uranus is a cold and dark planet spinning on its sides. It is made up of gas and icy materials. It has 13 known rings. It consists of hydrogen, helium and methane. It is only visited by one spacecraft, Voyager 2 and is still waiting to be explored.
